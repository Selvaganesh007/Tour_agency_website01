Explore Munnar and enjoy jeep safari across the tea city. The day tour covers Jeep safari (off road) to Kollukkumali from where guests can see the length and breadth of the formidable and lovely Western Ghats across the border of Tamilnadu and Kerala. It is at a height of 7200ft from the sea level. You can also embark on an exciting tea factory visit on the top of the mountain.

At the hilltop, get panoramic views of the surrounding villages of Munnar, plantations, and lush natural beauty.Experience adventure and capture amazing photographs of the emerald hills and the picturesque mountains. Explore the beauty of the tranquil plantations of Munnar with an exciting jeep safari in Kolukkumalai

Kolukkumalai tea is one of the premium tea in South India. Kolukkumalai is the World's Highest Elevation Tea Plantation. Our Kolukkumalai Tea has been a winner of the "Golden Leaf" award given by UPASI (The United Planters' Association of Southern India) for the best quality Tea. Contrary to the other usual CTC tea produced by the other plantations, we are using Orthodox methods to process the tea leaves.
